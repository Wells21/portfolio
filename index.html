Data has become an essential part of businesses and organizations,

and understanding it is crucial.

As a Data Analyst, I help organizations harness the power of data for their added advantage.

Its ethical that I begin my Thought Process from first understanding the vision and goal my stakeholder seeks to accomplish, of course which is my responsibility to make happen, a top most priority.


Secondly, "understanding the problem", which includes a thorough research and questioning, what needs to be done, achieved, and also serves as a guidemap / roadmap towards presenting the solution to the problem.


Thirdly, "understanding the Data". Does the data provides solution to the problem? If YES, I move to the cleaning phase and other processes that follows to arriving to a final solution. If NO, I ask more questions, preferably hold an interview to get more understanding of the data, also, in some cases its important I perform Feature Engineering.


After understanding the data, I then move on to the "analysis phase", which contains the processes of data cleaning, data manipulation, data exploration...e.t.c.


Which then, I present the findings and conclusion of my analysis, also providing recommendations for decision making.
